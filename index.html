Welcome to my website!

I am Vinod Pisharody and I am a physicist. I am interested in using gravitational waves as a probe to study the Universe we live in.

I did my undergraduate from St.Xavier's College, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India, and my masters in Physics from Harish-Chandra Research Institute, Allahabad (Prayagraj), Uttar Pradesh, India.
